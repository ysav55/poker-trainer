Incoming Zone — Behavior

  • Who lands here: Self-registered users with no school assignment (solo_student, or coached_student without a school)
  • Badge count: Gold counter always visible — acts as an inbox notification
  • Click a card: Opens user detail drawer (same as clicking a row) with "Assign to School" action prominent
  • "Assign All": Bulk-assign all incoming users to a school in one action
  • Once assigned: User moves from Incoming → their school's member list. Card disappears.
  • Empty state: When no incoming users, zone collapses to a single line: "✓ No pending users" with a green check
  • Staleness: Cards older than 7 days get a subtle red tint — nudge to act
